Daily rotating challenges that test different push-related skills with unique objectives and rewards.

- Accuracy Challenges
- Speed Push Tests
- Chain Push Combos

- Progressive difficulty scaling
- Varied objectives
- Special weekend challenges
- Holiday themed events

- Challenge point accumulation
- Monthly milestone rewards

- Situational awareness

- Special mechanic mastery
